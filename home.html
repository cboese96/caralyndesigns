A little about me


Hi and welcome to my website! My name is Caralyn - it’s nice to (digitally) meet you. I’m a recent grad from the University of Central Florida and I’m excited to grow and start my career in web design/digital media. During my time at UCF, I found my passion for UX/UI and front end web design along with graphic design. Over the past four years, I have mastered skills ranging from HTML, CSS, and multiple frameworks to adobe products and Microsoft Word.

Not only do I love creating beautifully designed websites that are user friendly and easy to navigate but, I also enjoy making the graphics that go along with them. Digital media isn’t just my career choice, but also my biggest hobby in life!

Besides all things web design, I also found that I have a strong skill-set in video editing, photoshop and writing (my dad always said I know how to write a good, engaging story)!

In my free time, I enjoy practicing my hand lettering and drawing skills and playing boardgames (my favorite being Catan). Oh, and of course loving up on my two adorable cats!

Feel free to check out the rest of my site and contact me! I am currently available for full time and freelance work.
